§ 01 · The Firm

From the boardroom to the courtroom, at the seats of government or at community association meetings, Sachs Sax Caplan Kaskel offers effective legal and business solutions to individuals, businesses, not-for-profit organizations, government agencies and community associations who call Florida home.
